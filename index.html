A model with a sharp silhouette, matching leather,
suede, and gum sole. White is stylish and black is
chic. This model was released in 1950, the year the
FIFA World Cup was held in Brazil, which is held once
every four years. Since the host country was Brazil,
the name comes from the representative Brazilian
music "samba". The grip and durability of the rubber
outsole and the foot protection of full-length leather
are features of shoes developed for futsal. The
moderate volume and minimal design make this
model the most popular among celebrities around the world.

Normcore is a term coined to describe a fashion style
that means "the ultimate in normality." It is created by
combining Normal and Hardcore, and has been a hot
topic since around 2014. Characterized by a style
that combines plain tops with denim and sneakers, it
is a fashion style that emphasizes how to express
yourself attractively with simple items, without being
influenced by trends. As you incorporate simple
items, it seems important to pay close attention to
the texture and design of the items you choose.
